About Me

I am Pauline Martinez, a freelance UX/UI designer. I specialize in User Testing, Prototyping, UX Writing, and enjoy solving user problems that are within the average users day to day. Interested in the growth of technology and design. The design process is my favorite part. User Testing and Prototyping allows me
to get out of my comfort zone and think more creatively. I am teachable, disciplined, and enjoy creating something that brings users comfort and confidence
to use. Aspiring to work in Fashion/Retail, Beauty, Hospitality, and Travel Fields. Location: Remote, United States or Hybrid within the greater Phoenix area in Arizona. I have been a certified designer for about half a year now.
